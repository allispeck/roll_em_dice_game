GAME RULES

  1. Each player can roll the dice as many times as he/she wants per round.
  2. If a 1 is rolled, you lose any points earned that round and your turn is over.
  3. You can press Hold at any point in a round to keep the points you have earned and end your turn.
  4. The first player to reach 100 points wins the game.
